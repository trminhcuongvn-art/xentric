# Script for LiveChat (ID: 014)

Your website is leaking customers while you sleep.

People land, get confused, and bounce because nobody answers the one question blocking the sale.

LiveChat fixes that: add a chat widget to your site, talk to visitors in real time, route questions to your team, and use AI-assisted replies to move faster.

For founders, creators, and small teams, it’s a practical way to turn “just browsing” into actual conversations—without forcing people to book a call or hunt for your email.

If your site gets traffic but not enough leads, start by fixing response time.
