# Product 001 — Customgpt

## Script English

**Framework: PAS for SaaS / AI tools**

**Hook (0-3s)**
Your support team answers the same five questions every day.

**Problem**
Most teams try to fix this with more tabs, more manual checklists, or another rushed workaround — and the workflow still breaks when volume increases.

**Agitate**
That means slower launches, missed follow-ups, and work that feels done but is impossible to measure.

**Solution**
Customgpt solves the bottleneck: Build a CustomGPT bot from your docs, FAQs, and knowledge base so customers and teammates can ask natural-language questions without digging through folders.

**Expected outcome**
Expected result: faster self-serve answers, fewer repetitive tickets, and a clearer path for onboarding new users.

**CTA**
If your help center is scattered across PDFs, Notion pages, and Google Docs, test CustomGPT before the next support backlog hits. Tap the link in bio to see whether Customgpt fits your workflow.
