# Script for Pictory AI (ID: 015)

Your best content is probably already sitting in a Google Doc, blog post, or webinar nobody’s watching.

Pictory AI turns long-form text or video into short, shareable videos with captions, stock visuals, music, and quick edits—without needing a full video team.

If you’re a founder, creator, or marketer, this means you can repurpose one idea into LinkedIn clips, TikToks, YouTube Shorts, and promo videos faster.

It’s not magic. You still need a good message. But Pictory helps you package that message into video way more efficiently.
