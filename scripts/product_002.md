# Product 002 — Reclaim AI

## Script English

**Framework: PAS for SaaS / AI tools**

**Hook (0-3s)**
Your calendar looks busy, but your most important work keeps getting pushed to tomorrow.

**Problem**
Most teams try to fix this with more tabs, more manual checklists, or another rushed workaround — and the workflow still breaks when volume increases.

**Agitate**
That means slower launches, missed follow-ups, and work that feels done but is impossible to measure.

**Solution**
Reclaim AI solves the bottleneck: Reclaim AI auto-schedules focus time, habits, and meetings around real availability instead of forcing you to rebuild your week manually.

**Expected outcome**
Expected result: protected deep-work blocks, fewer scheduling conflicts, and a calendar that adapts when priorities change.

**CTA**
If meetings are already eating your week, set this up before the next planning cycle. Tap the link in bio to see whether Reclaim AI fits your workflow.
